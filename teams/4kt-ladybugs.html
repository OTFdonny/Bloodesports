REDEFINING FEMALE ESPORTS

The 4KT Ladybugs represent the pinnacle of female excellence in African Call of Duty Mobile esports. As the premier all-female team under the 4KTrey Bloods banner, we're not just playing the game – we're shattering stereotypes and redefining what's possible for women in competitive gaming.

Our mission is to create a platform where female gamers can showcase their skills at the highest level, compete in major tournaments, and inspire the next generation of female esports athletes. We believe in skill, strategy, and sisterhood.

With multiple OGR (Onward Female Gaming Rankings) achievements under our belt, the Ladybugs have established themselves as a force to be reckoned with in the competitive scene.
