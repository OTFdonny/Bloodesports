THE FUTURE OF BLOOD ESPORTS

The Blood Hounds represent the developmental powerhouse of Blood Esports. As OTF's premier training and development team, our mission is to identify, nurture, and elevate the next generation of competitive gaming talent.

We're not just a team - we're an incubator for excellence. Our focus is on systematic player development, strategic experimentation, and building a solid foundation for players to transition into elite competition. The Blood Hounds are where potential meets opportunity, and raw talent is forged into championship material.

Operating under the OTF (Only The Family) division, we combine structured training with real competitive experience, preparing players for advancement to higher tiers within Blood Esports.
